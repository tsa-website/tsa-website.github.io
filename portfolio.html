Sierra

She is very intelligent and aware of her surroundings. She doesn’t fall into trouble easily. She is the one who keeps everyone together. She loves book reading and puzzle solving. Her strength is super speed and super hearing.

Samuel

The jokester of the group, sometimes doesn’t take things seriously and is fearless. He can easily get in trouble and is the most chill. He loves science and experiments. He has the ability to create beverages that can result in magical effects.

Aari

He is very wise and shy, even if he doesn’t like to show it. His rough exterior often throws people off, but inside he is compassionate like Sierra. He loves to do anything athletic and is the captain of every sports team. His super power is his strength.
